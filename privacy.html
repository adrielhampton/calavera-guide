Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Lake Calavera Preserve Field Guide (lakecalavera.com) handles information when you use this site.

What we collect

This site does not collect, store, or process any personal information. There are no user accounts, no forms, no cookies, no tracking pixels, and no analytics on this site. We do not collect your name, email address, location, device information, or any other identifying data.

Third-party services

This site loads species photos and biodiversity data from iNaturalist, operated by the California Academy of Sciences and National Geographic Society. When your browser retrieves photos and data from iNaturalist, your request is subject to iNaturalist's Privacy Policy. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of iNaturalist.

This site also loads fonts from Google Fonts. Font requests are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Hosting

This site is served by a third-party hosting provider. Standard web server logs (such as IP addresses and page requests) may be retained by the hosting provider in accordance with their own privacy and data retention policies. We do not have access to or use these logs.

Embedding

This field guide may be embedded on other websites, including the Preserve Calavera website. Those websites may have their own privacy practices, cookies, and data collection independent of this guide. Please refer to the privacy policies of any site that embeds this guide.

Children's privacy

This site does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users.

California residents

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have certain rights regarding their personal information. Because this site does not collect personal information, there is no personal information to access, delete, or opt out of selling. If you have questions, please contact us at the address below.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy: lakecalavera@adrielhampton.com
